Terms

ATM
Asynchronous Transfer Mode - Broadband technology to video, sound and ICT transmission. ATM most often, is used in ADSL technology.
DSL
Digital Subscriber Line - Broadband technology of connect to the internet, used in network above 10 kHz. Uses existing telecommunication wiring.
